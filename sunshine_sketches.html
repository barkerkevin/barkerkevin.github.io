Take a walk through the seasons of a lifelong affair of the heart.

Spring opens a fresh, new and exciting love, followed by summer which gives warmth and growth to the union. Autumn brings the closing of the barn door, the sweeping of the porch, and flight to the safety of the hearth, followed by the darkness and acceptance of winter where every glint of sun on snow is taken as the coming of spring.

A Fine Romance is the story of a loving marriage which found constant renewal throughout 60 years of turbulent living. Told in a series of love poems themed on the changing seasons, and annotated with masterful oil paintings depicting the same, the book makes superb fireside reading - ideal for those long winter nights when the soul turns inward and yearns for the rays of hope.

| Special limited print edition featuring original oils by esteemed coast painter Shannon Barker |

Terry Barker | Best known for his long running Sunshine Sketches columns in the Coast Reporter newspaper in Sechelt, B.C., and his books about British Columbia's west coast, Terry Barker is a lifelong poet and husband of Connie Barker, his muse of 60 years. He never stopped writing her love poems. In A Fine Romance, he shares these intensely personal words with his readers.

Shannon Barker | Retired from the business world for over 10 years, Shannon took up fine arts, working in acrylics and ceramics. She has studied with various mentors and exhibited in several galleries and group shows around Vancouver. She lives with her husband Robin on the Sunshine Coast of British Columbia.
