About Terry Barker | A full time communications trainer and lecturer for over 35 years, Terry Barker published his first book, Boss Talk, in 1983. Primarly problem/solution based, it comprises the experiences of countless managers and supervisors across a wide spectrum of industries. Boss Talk has sold sold more than 30,000 copies around the world, and is still widely used in a variety of training courses. His second book, published six years later, was entitledThe5 Minute Police Officer. Again concerned with communication skills, it was designed for police training courses and is in continual demand among law enforcement personnel worldwide.
